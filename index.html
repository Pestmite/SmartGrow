About This Project

The SmartGrow irrigation system is a fully automatic sprinkler that uses real-world forecast data, soil moisture sensors, a custom machine-learning inspired algorithm and 15+ tunable variables to water your plants. Includes a fully 3d-printed structure and costs under $20 to make in bulk.

Created over 100+ hours for my grade 9 Science Fair.
