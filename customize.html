Tomato plants (Solanum lycopersicum) are sun-loving, warm-season crops grown for their nutritious fruit. They thrive in well-drained, slightly acidic soil and need consistent care. Available in determinate (bush) and indeterminate (vine) varieties, they are used fresh, cooked, or processed but are vulnerable to pests, diseases, and irregular watering.
